About Us

Katrivan Business Enterprises Limited is a diversified enterprise proudly based in Kayunga District, Uganda. We serve both local and foreign markets, delivering top-quality agricultural products and business solutions. We are committed to empowering our community through multiple pillars: quality livestock and crop farming, wholesale agro-products for export, accessible financial services, and reliable communication solutions. In partnership with esteemed collaborators like Bamul Enterprises and Kandrenah Group of Companies, we deliver excellence across the agricultural value chain — from farm to global markets. Katrivan is your one-stop shop for quality, trust, and service.

Frequently Asked Questions

What types of livestock do you sell?+

We sell a wide range of healthy livestock including cows (dairy and beef), goats (meat and dairy), pigs, hens (local and improved breeds), and turkeys. All our animals are well-bred, vaccinated, and raised in quality conditions. We serve both local customers and international buyers. Visit us in Kayunga or contact us to check current availability.

Do you deliver products to foreign/international markets?+

Yes! We export agricultural products to international markets. Our export-ready products include coffee, maize, bananas, avocados, pineapples, and more. All export produce meets international quality and safety standards. We handle bulk sourcing, quality inspection, and logistics through reliable supply chain partners.

How can I request a price quote?+

Getting a quote is easy! Simply visit our Contact section, fill out the form with details of what you're looking for (product type, quantity, location), and we'll get back to you with competitive pricing. You can also call us directly at 0702 013 189 or WhatsApp us for faster responses.

What mobile money services do you offer?+

We offer full mobile money services including sending and receiving money via MTN Mobile Money and Airtel Money, cash withdrawals, bill payments, and agent registration services. We also assist with SIM card registration for all major networks in Uganda.

Can I buy a phone on credit?+

Yes! We offer smartphones and feature phones on flexible credit/installment plans. You can pay in installments over an agreed period. Terms and conditions apply — visit our shop in Kayunga District to discuss available options and find a plan that works for you.

How do I send money overseas through Katrivan?+

Our overseas money transfer service is fast, secure, and reliable. Visit our location in Kayunga District with your recipient's details and the amount you wish to send. We process international remittances through trusted partner networks. Contact us for current exchange rates and transfer fees.

What are your working hours?+

We are open Monday through Sunday, from 7:00 AM to 7:00 PM. Whether you need livestock, crops, financial services, or phones — visit us any day of the week during these hours. You can also reach us by phone or WhatsApp outside working hours.
